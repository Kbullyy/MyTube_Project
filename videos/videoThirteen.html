Toyota LOWRIDER TRUCK on 3 Wheels! Classic Car Cruise in Wilmington, California

Lowriders, Classic Cars & Vintage Automobiles swarmed the streets for this Lowriding Car Show
and Car Cruise in Wilmington, Los Angeles, California after the 2024 Lowrider Super Show. A
1964 Chevrolet Impala was doing burnouts, and there were Lowriders hitting switches; bouncing,
hopping and cruising in 3 wheel motion alongside clean Classic Cars! A Lowrider Hopping Competition
took place between a 64 and a 62 Impala, with hopping lowriders hitting back bumper, putting on a show
for the car enthusiasts and classic car culture fans!
